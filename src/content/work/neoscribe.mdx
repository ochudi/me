## Context

Clinical documentation eats clinician time, and the easy version of an AI fix
makes the dangerous trade of speed for trust. NeoScribe is an internal tool at
Plural Health built the other way around: the model drafts, the clinician
decides.

## Approach

The pipeline turns a clinical conversation into a structured draft note. That
draft never becomes a record on its own. It enters a review workflow where a
clinician edits and approves, so the human stays accountable for every line
and nothing is published unchecked. The interface is built to make that review
fast rather than to hide it.

## Outcome

The product lead called it impressively intuitive, and the demo carried itself.
As an internal tool the specifics stay private; more is available on request.
