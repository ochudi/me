## Context

Ife Afolabi is a TikTok creator with an audience of four million. The brief was
deliberately small: one page that works as a home base, links out to the
platforms, and never falls over when a video takes off and tens of thousands
of people tap the link in the same hour.

## Approach

One page, one voice. The whole site is a single statically rendered route on
Next.js, served from Vercel's edge, so a traffic spike is just more cache hits
rather than more load. Type and motion carry the personality; there is no
heavy hero video to slow the first paint on a phone, which is where almost all
of the audience lands.

## Outcome

It shipped fast, and the client was happy enough to clear the balance before
the site even went live. It now sits behind the link in every bio, ready for
the next viral post.
